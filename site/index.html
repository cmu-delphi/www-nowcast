How to interact with ILI Nearby?

Clicking on the information button will show useful information regarding ILI Nearby.

Clicking on the refresh button will refresh ILI Nearby's web application.

Clicking on the question button will show useful help and tips regarding ILI Nearby.

Clicking on the layer's button will show ILI Nearby's map layers.

After clicking on a map area, click on show details → to view the region's detailed values.

ILI Nearby provides geographically detailed real-time estimates (“nowcasts”) of Influenza-Like-Illness in the United States. It fuses estimates from a variety of information sources, both digital and traditional, as well as from computational and crowdsourced models of epidemic dynamics. ILI Nearby has been operational continuously since October 2016 and reflects ongoing advances being made by the Delphi epidemic forecasting research group at Carnegie Mellon University. It has a historical track record of high accuracy as measured against CDC’s “finalized” estimates available after the end of each season.
